Lionel Messi

Lionel Andrés is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and the Argentina national team.

FIFA World Cup

Lionel Messi has an exceptional FIFA World Cup career, culminating in Argentina’s historic victory in 2022. He won the Golden Ball twice, in 2014 and 2022, for being the tournament’s best player, and he holds the record for the most World Cup appearances.

UEFA Champions League

Lionel Messi has an outstanding record in UEFA competitions, particularly in the UEFA Champions League, where he won the title four times with Barcelona (2006, 2009, 2011, 2015). He holds several records, including most Champions League goals for a single club (120 for Barcelona) and most goals in the group stage (80)

Copa Del Rey

Lionel Messi has had an impressive career in the Copa América, winning the tournament twice—in 2021 and 2024. He holds the record for most assists in Copa América history with 18 assists and has scored 13 goals across 35 matches
